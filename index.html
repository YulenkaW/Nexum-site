SERVICES

Three tiers to match how carefully you want to move.

Tier 1

Readiness & Architecture

A low-risk starting point designed to create clarity and reduce uncertainty.

Includes
  • Review of existing systems, data, and workflows
  • Identification of realistic automation or AI opportunities
  • Architecture diagrams and integration approach
  • Build vs buy recommendations
  • Vendor and tooling guidance (vendor-neutral)
  • Security, compliance, and cost considerations
Outcome
A clear, documented plan — or a clear decision not to proceed.
Tier 2

Proof of Concept

Small, focused solutions delivered quickly to validate value.

Examples
  • Internal document search or knowledge assistant
  • Workflow automation or decision support
  • Data summarization or classification
  • Lightweight internal tools for teams
Principles
  • Narrow scope
  • Fast delivery
  • Human-in-the-loop
  • Easy to stop or evolve
Outcome
A working solution that proves feasibility without long-term commitment.
Tier 3

Implementation & Integration

Production-ready systems designed to last.

Includes
  • Secure deployment (cloud or hybrid)
  • Integration with existing platforms and data
  • Monitoring, logging, and access controls
  • Documentation and handoff
  • Optional ongoing support
Outcome
A maintainable system aligned with your internal capabilities.
